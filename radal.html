The Challenge & The Solution

Bridging Fragmented Health Tracks

The Problem

Health tracking tools today are overwhelmingly disjointed. Users are forced to switch between calorie logs, training programs, mindfulness players, and smart sleep trackers.

  • Fragmented Data Silos: Sleep data is locked in one app while diet plans reside in another, preventing a holistic understanding of health.
  • Generic Advice: Many apps generate static plans that fail to reflect immediate physical parameters or specific dietary needs.
  • Friction & Fatigue: Navigating multiple subscription charges and user interfaces reduces long-term user consistency and tracking adherence.

The Solution

RADAL bridges these gaps by consolidating core tracking workflows under a cohesive AI core. It offers dynamic recommendations based on collective profile data.

  • Unified Wellness Model: Evaluates health parameters globally. Sleep indicators automatically modify fitness difficulty recommendations the following morning.
  • Cognitive AI Insights: Powered by Claude 3.5 Sonnet to provide precise, highly contextual recommendations based on the user's direct inputs.
  • Structured Prototypes: Uses AWS PartyRock's interactive layout to iterate rapidly on layout ideas and generate real-time feedback loops.
